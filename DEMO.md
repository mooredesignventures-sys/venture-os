# DEMO (8 Steps)

1. Open [Login](http://localhost:3000/login) and enter the app.
2. Open [Nodes](http://localhost:3000/app/nodes) and click **Load Demo Data**.
3. In [Nodes](http://localhost:3000/app/nodes), create/edit a node.
4. In node detail, add a relationship to another node.
5. Open [Views](http://localhost:3000/app/views) and check Decisions/Requirements/Business.
6. Commit a node in [Nodes](http://localhost:3000/app/nodes), then verify [Audit](http://localhost:3000/app/audit).
7. Export a bundle from [Nodes](http://localhost:3000/app/nodes) using **Export Bundle (JSON)**.
8. Click **Reset (clear all local data)** in [Nodes](http://localhost:3000/app/nodes).
